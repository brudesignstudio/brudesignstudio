Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Bru Design Studio ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us. When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name (optional)
  • The topic you select and the message you write

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information. We do not collect payment information through this website.

How we use your information

We use the details you submit for a single purpose: to respond to your enquiry, answer your questions, and discuss working together. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information, and we will not add you to marketing lists without your clear consent.

How your message reaches us

Our contact form is processed by a third-party form-delivery service, which securely passes your submission to our email inbox. Your information is handled according to that provider's own privacy and security practices and is used only to deliver your message to us.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Some resources, such as web fonts, may be loaded from third-party providers (for example, Google Fonts), which can receive standard technical information like your IP address as part of serving those files. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy first.

How we protect your information

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and limit access to it. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and only keep it for as long as needed to respond to you and maintain our records.

Your choices and rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you have shared with us at any time. To make a request, simply contact us through the form on our website and we will respond promptly.

Children's privacy

This website is intended for business owners and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please reach out through our contact form and we'll be glad to help.
